CTU Danao Students Showcase Talents at Annual CTU Night

By Mike Johnson - October 3, 2024

The Cebu Technological University (CTU) Danao Campus recently held its much-awaited annual CTU Night, where students displayed their impressive talents in a vibrant showcase of music, dance, and various artistic performances.
